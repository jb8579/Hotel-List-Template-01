What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit websites. They are often used to make websites work more efficiently and to transmit information to website operators. Cookies can be temporary (session cookies) or permanent (persistent cookies).

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to collect information about how you use our website and to improve your user experience. For example, cookies allow you to set your preferences, perform analytics, and deliver personalized content. In most cases, this information is anonymized and is not used to identify you personally.

What types of cookies do we use?

1. Necessary cookies: These cookies are essential to create the basic functions of our website.

2. Management cookies: these cookies contain information about visitors to our site. We help you improve or improve our site.

3. Functional cookies: These cookies allow the site to remember your schools and previous settings to create a personalized user experience.

How can you control cookies?

You can generate and refuse cookies in your browser settings. Be careful, however, disabling cookies may affect the functions of our website. For more information on managing cookies, consult your browser's privacy settings.
